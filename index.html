About Me

Hi! I am Parisha Jain. I have found my ikigai in Arts. I am still exploring myself everyday. I observe things which usually people pass by and I perceive the world around me in a different way . I have completed my schooling from Fountainhead School, Surat. I have a keen interest in photographing, making art and writing poems. I got the courage to make this website by reading the book “Show your work” by Austin Kleon .You will find glimpse of me through my work.I have tried putting all what I have explored in the field of arts. I strongly believe in the philosophy “I think, therefore I am” and. “Live and let live”
